About Us

Lucy Industrial Co.., Ltd (LIC) is a leading magnets manufacturer and exporter in Taiwan.

We supply a variety of magnets more then 20 years, specialized in the high energy, thin wall magnets.

Our in house multi-poles fixtures production ability can support the engineers designs.

LIC expand the service to HB & PM stepping motor and Gear motor years ago, the unit quality and short delivery time are so appreciated by the customers.

All the material are meet the RoHs standard and production according to ISO 9001.
